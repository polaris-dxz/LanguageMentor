**目标:**
- 用英语完成看房与租房沟通（Complete a flat viewing and rental discussion in English）

**挑战:**
1. 与房东/中介打招呼并说明来看房  
   Greet the landlord/agent and say you are here for the viewing
2. 询问租金、押金、租期与入住时间  
   Ask about rent, deposit, lease length, and move-in date
3. 询问水电、物业及房屋规则（如宠物、转租）  
   Ask about utilities, management, and rules (e.g. pets, subletting)
4. 表达对房子的喜好或顾虑  
   Say what you like or don’t like about the property
5. 决定是否租下并说明下一步（签约、拿钥匙）  
   Decide whether to take it and clarify next steps (contract, keys)
