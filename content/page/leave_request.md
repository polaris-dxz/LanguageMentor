**目标:**
- 用英语向领导/HR 请假并说明原因与日期（Request leave from your manager/HR in English）

**挑战:**
1. 礼貌地提出需要请假并说明是病假/年假/事假  
   Politely state that you need to request leave (sick / annual / personal)
2. 简要说明请假原因（生病、家事、旅行等）  
   Briefly explain the reason (illness, family, travel) in a professional way
3. 明确说出请假日期与天数  
   Clearly state the dates and number of days
4. 回答关于工作交接或证明材料的问题  
   Answer questions about handover or required documentation
5. 确认审批结果或后续步骤（如提交申请、通知同事）  
   Confirm approval or next steps (e.g. submitting a form, informing colleagues)
