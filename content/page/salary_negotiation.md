**目标:**
- 用英语完成一次专业的薪资谈判（Complete a professional salary negotiation in English）

**挑战:**
1. 礼貌地提出希望讨论薪资与福利  
   Politely state that you would like to discuss salary and benefits
2. 询问薪资构成（基本工资、奖金、福利等）  
   Ask about the compensation structure (base pay, bonus, benefits)
3. 得体地表达自己的期望或顾虑  
   Express your expectations or concerns in a professional way
4. 对对方的还价或替代方案做出回应  
   Respond to counter-offers or alternative proposals (e.g. equity, flexibility)
5. 做出接受、考虑或婉拒的决定并收尾  
   Accept, ask for time to consider, or decline politely and close the conversation
